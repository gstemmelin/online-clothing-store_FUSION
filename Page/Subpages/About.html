¿What is "FUSION"?

"FUSION" is a merchandising brand that focuses on designs for shirts specifically. This is a personal project, created by one person.



¿How we operate?

We operate with the prepayment system, when ordering a t-shirt a deposit is requested to begin its production. Once the production of the t-shirt is completed, it will be sent to the address previously selected by the buyer.
